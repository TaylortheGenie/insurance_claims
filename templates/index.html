Introduction

Welcome to the vehicle insurance claim prediction application. This application uses machine learning to predict whether or not a policyholder will file a claim for vehicle insurance. To use the application, simply fill out the form provided.

Once you have entered this information, click on the 'Predict' button and the application will determine whether or not you are likely to file a claim for vehicle insurance.

Kindly note that this application is not a substitute for professional advice! If you have any questions about your insurance coverage, please contact your insurance agent.
